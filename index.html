💡 Underwriting Tips
SR-22 Clients ▾
Route SR-22 clients to Dairyland or Bristol West first. Most standard carriers will decline or heavily surcharge. Always confirm filing state and duration required. SR-22 filings typically last 3 years.
Young Drivers (Under 25) ▾
Young drivers see 30–50% surcharges at most carriers. Ask about Good Student Discount (3.0+ GPA) and Defensive Driver courses — both are underutilized discounts. Lemonade and Progressive tend to be more competitive here.
Prior Lapse in Coverage ▾
A lapse of 30+ days may result in declination from preferred carriers. Bristol West accepts lapses. Always ask how long the lapse was and the reason — new car purchases or moves can sometimes be explained away.
Homeowner Discounts ▾
Homeownership (not just bundling) is a standalone discount at Elephant and several other carriers. Always ask even if the client isn't looking to bundle home insurance.
Financed / Leased Vehicles ▾
Lienholders and lessors require comprehensive and collision coverage. Confirm deductible limits — many lenders cap deductibles at $500. GAP coverage is separate and worth mentioning to financed buyers.
